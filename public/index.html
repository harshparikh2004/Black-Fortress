About us

Vault-Tec is a demo security-focused app showcasing best practices in authentication, authorization, and RBAC. Built with Node.js, Express, JWT, and MongoDB.

  • Hashed passwords with bcrypt
  • JWT via HttpOnly cookies
  • Role-based access (Admin/User)
  • Rate limiting and account lockout
